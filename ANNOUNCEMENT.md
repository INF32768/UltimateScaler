But the reality is harsh.

### I can no longer continue for love alone

- My cat is ill, and the medical expenses have become a considerable burden
- Home devices are continuously breaking, and repair costs have strained my household finances
- My father's business collaboration with a major server manufacturer fell through at the last moment

The ideals of the open-source community are beautiful, but a developer's stomach is not filled by the number of Stars.

**I need to survive, and I also need this project to survive.**

## 📋 Specific Change Plan

### 1. Code Repository

- This repository will be archived as **read-only on April 2, 2026, at 00:00 UTC+8**
- All future code updates will no longer be public
- The existing code will be retained as a historical archive but will no longer accept PRs or Issues

### 2. Release Channels

- All new mod versions will only be released on **CurseForge** and **Modrinth**
- Release files will be **obfuscated and encrypted**
- We will integrate a **paid verification system** and **anti-piracy protection mechanism** within the mod

### 3. Anti-Piracy Statement

For any attempts to crack, reverse-engineer, or redistribute this mod without authorization, we will:

- Identify and ban relevant devices through technical means
- Report infringing content to relevant platforms
- Reserve the right to take further legal action

**We have zero tolerance for this.**
